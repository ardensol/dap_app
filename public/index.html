How It Works
We're making it ridiculously easy to become the best dressed man in the room.  Place your order for a Dapper Box and we'll use your body profile to create a tailored package of garments that fits your style.  Only pay for what you keep and just send back the rest.
Individually Styled
Our stylists will use your fit profile and personal preferences to create a package of garments tailored to you.
Premium Quality Garments
From 100% Cashmere sweaters to stylish ties, your package will range from the best of men's basics to more dapper essentials.
Perfect Fit
Don't worry about getting an ill fitting garment, we use your unique body profile to ensure that your garment fits before we ship.
Easy Shopping

Once you order your Dapper Box a Stylist will put together your first package of clothes containing 5-7 items.  Packages typically arrive 5 days after you place your order.  You’ll have three days to choose your garments and return the package with our prepaid shipping label.

FAQ

Is this a subscription service?
No, you only receive a package whenever you request one.  You won't get stuck with a recurring bill.

How much are the garments?
We provide high quality products.  The prices are around $65 per piece, but may be lower or higher depending on the product.

Who pays for shipping?
We'll pay for shipping.  You pay for the clothes you keep, free shipping both ways.  However, if you don't choose any products you will be charged a $20 styling fee.
